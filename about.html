About Voltage Electric Hawaii

Maui's trusted electrical contractor since 2009.

Our Story

Built on Maui, Built for Maui

Rafael Aquino grew up in Wailuku and started his electrical career as an apprentice at 18, working on commercial projects across Oahu before returning home to start Voltage Electric in 2009. He saw that Maui's electrical infrastructure was aging and that the island's push toward renewable energy was creating demand for electricians who understood both traditional systems and modern solar/EV technology.

Today, Voltage Electric employs six licensed electricians and has completed over 900 projects across Maui. We're particularly proud of our work supporting Maui's transition to clean energy — over 200 of our projects have involved solar integration or EV infrastructure.
